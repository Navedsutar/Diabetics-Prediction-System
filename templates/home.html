Our Mission

Diabetes affects over 463 million people worldwide, and nearly half of cases go undiagnosed. Our team came together to create this prediction system as part of our academic research to demonstrate the potential of AI in early disease detection.

We believe that accessible, accurate risk assessment tools can empower individuals to take proactive steps toward better health. This project combines medical expertise with cutting-edge technology to provide a service that's both scientifically rigorous and easy to use.
